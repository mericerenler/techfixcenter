Error Codes Explained

Don't let random numbers confuse you. Here is what they mean and how to fix them.

Advertisement

  • Windows Update Error 0x80070002

    Means "File Not Found." Usually caused by missing update files. Easy 3-step fix.

  • HTTP Error 503: Service Unavailable

    The server is busy or down. It's not your internet. try refreshing in 5 minutes.

  • PS5 Error CE-108255-1

    Game crashed. Often solved by disabling HDCP or reinstalling the game.

  • iPhone Error 4013

    Connection issue during update. Try a different USB cable or port.

  • Blue Screen: DRIVER_IRQL_NOT_LESS_OR_EQUAL

    A driver is crashing. Usually WiFi or Graphics driver needs an update.
