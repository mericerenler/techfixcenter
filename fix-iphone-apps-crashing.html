Fix iPhone Apps Crashing

Updated Feb 13, 2026
Advertisement

The Problem: You tap an app icon (like Instagram or Maps), it opens for a split second, and then closes immediately.

The Short Answer: The app file is corrupt, or it needs an update to match your iOS version.

Quick Fixes

  • Method 1: Update the App
  • Method 2: Offload the App
  • Method 3: Check Storage

Method 1: Update the App

  1. Open the App Store.
  2. Tap your profile icon.
  3. Scroll down to see updates.
  4. Tap Update next to the crashing app.
Advertisement

Method 2: Offload the App

This reinstalls the app without deleting your data (logins, saves, etc).

  1. Go to Settings > General > iPhone Storage.
  2. Find the crashing app.
  3. Tap Offload App.
  4. Wait a moment, then tap Reinstall App.

Method 3: Restart Phone

Simple but effective. It clears the RAM.

  • Turn your phone off and on again.
