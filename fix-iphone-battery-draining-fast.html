Fix iPhone Battery Draining Fast

Updated Feb 13, 2026
Advertisement

The Problem: Your iPhone dies before the end of the day, or you watch the percentage drop every minute.

The Short Answer: If your battery health is above 80%, this is usually caused by "Background App Refresh" or high screen brightness.

Quick Fixes

  • Method 1: Check Battery Usage
  • Method 2: Turn Off Background Refresh
  • Method 3: Reduce Motion & Brightness

Method 1: Check Battery Usage

Find the culprit.

  1. Go to Settings > Battery.
  2. Scroll down to see the list.
  3. If an app like Facebook or TikTok says "Background Activity", it is killing your battery silently.
Advertisement

Method 2: Turn Off Background Refresh

Stop apps from updating when you aren't using them.

  1. Go to Settings > General > Background App Refresh.
  2. Turn it OFF completely, or disable it for social media apps.

Method 3: Reduce Motion & Brightness

The screen is the biggest power user.

  • Keep brightness at 50% or auto.
  • Go to Settings > Accessibility > Motion and turn on Reduce Motion. This turns off the fancy zoom effects.
