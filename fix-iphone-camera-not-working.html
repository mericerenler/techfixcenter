Fix iPhone Camera Not Working

Updated Feb 13, 2026
Advertisement

The Problem: You open the Camera app, and the preview is just black. Or the app freezes instantly.

The Short Answer: If the flashlight still works, this is a software crash. If the flashlight is also broken, your rear camera hardware is likely disconnected.

Quick Fixes

  • Method 1: Check the Flashlight (The Test)
  • Method 2: Force Close App
  • Method 3: Reset Settings

Method 1: Check the Flashlight (The Test)

The camera and flash are engaged on the same circuit.

  • Swipe down to Control Center.
  • Tap the Flashlight icon.
  • If it turns on: Good! It's just a software glitch. Proceed to Method 2.
  • If it doesn't turn on: Your camera module is physically broken or loose. You need a repair shop.
Advertisement

Method 2: Force Close App

  1. Swipe up from the bottom and hold to see open apps.
  2. Swipe the Camera app up to close it.
  3. Re-open it.

Method 3: Switch Cameras

Sometimes switching between Front and Back mode unfreezes the shutter.

  • Open Camera.
  • Tap the Rotate Camera icon repeatedly.
